Support Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question about LASER-web? Find quick answers to the most common questions from our students and faculty below.

Click on the "Login" button on the homepage. You'll be automatically redirected to the Microsoft account login page. Enter your official credentials to access your dashboard.

Visit the Microsoft account login page and click on "Forgot password?" Follow the on-screen instructions to verify your identity and reset your password.

Go to the "Help" section in the navigation bar and use the Contact Us option to reach the school's support team. Alternatively, you can leave a message using the provided form.

Navigate to the "Departments" section in the main menu to explore all subject areas and view detailed information about each specific department.

Access the "Resources" section, then click on the M.A.P. Portal to view your grades and other academic details securely.

D.R.I.V.E. is a dedicated resource portal located in the "Resources" section. It provides access to necessary files for information, circulation, and submission. Simply click on D.R.I.V.E. to find the materials you need.

Go to the "About" section in the navigation menu and click on "News" to stay informed about the latest announcements, updates, and upcoming events.

Visit the "Offices" section in the main menu to view information about the Principal's Office, Guidance Office, Clinic, Records Office, and School-Based Management (SBM).

Yes! The LASER-web site is fully responsive and optimized for seamless use on most mobile devices, smartphones, and tablets.

If you experience any technical difficulties, please visit the "Help" section and use the Contact Us option to get in touch with our technical support team.
